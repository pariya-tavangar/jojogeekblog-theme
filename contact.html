Frequently Asked Questions

How often do you post new content? +

I aim to publish new blog posts every Tuesday and Thursday. Occasionally, I'll post bonus content on weekends if I have something special to share. You can always subscribe to the newsletter to get notified when new content is published.

Do you accept guest posts or collaborations? +

Yes! I love collaborating with other creators who share my passion for 90s nostalgia and geek culture. If you have an idea for a guest post or collaboration, please use the contact form and select "Collaboration" as the subject.

Can I suggest topics for future posts? +

Absolutely! I'm always looking for new ideas and perspectives. If there's a specific 90s topic, game, or piece of technology you'd like me to cover, please use the contact form and select "Content Suggestion" as the subject.

Do you have a content schedule or editorial calendar? +

I maintain a rough editorial calendar that plans content about a month in advance. While I don't publish the full calendar publicly, newsletter subscribers occasionally get sneak peeks at upcoming topics. Major series or special content is usually announced in advance on social media.

Can I use your content on my own website? +

All original content on CyberCozy is protected by copyright. However, I'm generally happy to allow excerpts with proper attribution and links back to the original post. For full republishing or commercial use, please contact me first to discuss permissions.
