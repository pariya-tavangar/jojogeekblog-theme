About CyberCozy

Your digital campfire for 90s nostalgia, geek culture, and cozy vibes

Welcome to My Digital Time Capsule

Hello there! I'm Alex, a 90s kid who never quite left the era of dial-up internet, Tamagotchis, and the original PlayStation. CyberCozy is my love letter to the technology, games, and culture that shaped a generation.

Growing up in the 90s, I witnessed the birth of the internet as we know it, the rise of gaming from 8-bit to 3D, and the golden age of anime on Western television. These experiences shaped who I am today and inspired me to create this space where we can celebrate all things nostalgic and geeky.

When I'm not writing about retro tech or playing classic games, I work as a web developer—which explains both my love for the early web and my ability to build this site from scratch! I believe that understanding where we came from helps us appreciate where we're going, especially in the fast-paced world of technology.

CyberCozy isn't just about looking backward, though. It's about finding the connections between past and present, discovering how the innovations of yesterday influence the tech of today, and building a community of like-minded individuals who appreciate the cozy, sometimes quirky, side of geek culture.

My Journey

1994

The Beginning

Received my first console - the Super Nintendo Entertainment System. Played Super Mario World until my thumbs were sore and fell in love with gaming.

1999

Internet Awakening

Experienced dial-up internet for the first time. The sound of connecting still brings a tear to my eye. Started building my first Geocities website.

2005

College Years

Studied Computer Science while maintaining a blog about retro gaming on the side. Started collecting vintage tech and games seriously.

2018

CyberCozy Born

Launched CyberCozy as a passion project. What started as a small blog grew into a community of fellow 90s kids and retro enthusiasts.

2023

Today

Continuing to explore the intersection of nostalgia and modern technology. Working on a book about the cultural impact of 90s tech.

What We Stand For

Nostalgia with Purpose

We don't just look back for the sake of it. We explore how the past informs the present and find meaningful connections between then and now.

Community First

CyberCozy is built around the idea that shared memories and interests create the strongest bonds. Everyone is welcome at our digital campfire.

Handcrafted Content

Every article, review, and guide is carefully researched and written with love. No AI-generated content, just genuine passion and expertise.

Playful Exploration

We believe that curiosity and playfulness are essential to learning and growth. Our content reflects this spirit of joyful discovery.
